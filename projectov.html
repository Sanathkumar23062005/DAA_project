What is Ohara: The City of Dreams?

Ohara: City of Dreams is an urban planning project aimed at solving key city challenges through smart infrastructure, traffic management, and sustainable development. The goal is to create a dynamic, modern city that provides solutions to problems such as congestion, emergency vehicle routing, and smart public transport systems.

Findings from Research

  • Congestion Trends: Increasing traffic jams due to poor routing and signal management.
  • Public Transit Gaps: Inconsistent schedules causing underutilization of buses and trains.
  • Parking Issues: Time wasted searching for parking spots in busy areas.
  • Emergency Delays: Lack of effective routing for emergency vehicles.
  • Environmental Impact: High emissions from idling vehicles in congested zones.

City Design Reflection

The design of Ohara focuses on providing sustainable and smart solutions. The following areas were identified as primary objectives:

  1. Traffic Management: Implementing algorithms to monitor, predict, and reduce congestion in real-time.
  2. Emergency Vehicle Routing: Prioritized and dynamic routing to ensure timely access for emergency services.
  3. Smart Parking: Optimizing parking spaces through real-time data and sensors.
  4. Public Transport Scheduling: Providing optimal schedules for better connectivity and reduced waiting times.

Technologies Behind the System

  • IoT Sensors: Sensors embedded throughout the city to collect data on traffic and road conditions.
  • Big Data Analytics: Used to analyze traffic patterns and adjust routes or toll rates dynamically.
  • AI & Machine Learning: Algorithms that optimize transport routes, parking, and traffic management.
  • Smart Infrastructure: Traffic lights, parking sensors, and real-time data systems that adapt to changing conditions.
